Our Mission

Do you as a student find it very difficult to cope up with studies in schools and colleges? Or are you in need of a parttime job?
Our website helps to connect students who are ready to teach other students in your locality . This enables you as a student to not only learn but also to teach thereby adding to the educational community. So our primary objective is to help students by giving a one to one online or offline session at a cheap rate.


How our tutors are selected ?

The eligibility of the tutors will be thoroughly verified through interviews by presenting the most commonly asked questions related to the topic the tutor signed up for. The qualifications will be looked into and legitimacy of the documents will also be verified. The interviews will be conducted by the administrators itself on a one-to-one basis in real time .


Safety

We also ensure safety of both tutor and student using live location feature .Moreover, we have included an alert button option. This helps us track their whereabouts and prevent misuse.


Marketplace

This is also an interface that lets students post assignments, papers, notes etc. It features a marketplace to sell old study references or instruments that are no longer needed.
